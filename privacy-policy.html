Privacy Policy

App Name: भगवद् गीता – Bhagavad Geeta

This Privacy Policy explains how the app “भगवद् गीता – Bhagavad Geeta” (“the app”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) handles user information.

Information Collection and Use

We do not collect, store, or share any personal or sensitive user information. The app works completely offline and is designed to provide access to Bhagavad Geeta content.

The app allows users to:

  • Read the complete Bhagavad Geeta
  • Listen to Bhagavad Geeta audio
  • Search chapters and verses by typing
  • Search chapters and verses using voice input
  • Bookmark favorite verses
  • Share verses with others

Audio Permission (RECORD_AUDIO)

The app uses the android.permission.RECORD_AUDIO permission only to enable voice-based search functionality.

  • No audio is recorded or stored
  • No voice data is saved on the device or any server
  • No voice data is shared with any third party
  • Voice input is used temporarily only for search purposes

Data Storage

Any bookmarks or user preferences are stored locally on the user’s device. We do not use any external servers or databases.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share any user data with third parties.

Children’s Privacy

This app is suitable for all age groups. We do not knowingly collect any personal information.

Third-Party Services

The app does not use any third-party services that collect personal or sensitive user data.
